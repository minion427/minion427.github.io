Best Times

The best time I had recently was when I went to the theatre in Charlotte with my friends to watch the musical 'Hamilton'. Although it's very popular show in the states, it was my first time hearing about it. I think maybe it's because the historical backgrounds of the musical plots, and lots of hiphop-based lyrics that's very difficult to translate in other language, to be performed in other culture. I've watched the movie Hamilton, which is the recording of the broadway show in Disney Plus first, so I could understand the basic plots of it. It was especially special memory to me because my friends explained to me the American backgrounds and cultures in the plot that I couldn't understand.

The most interesting part of that musical was that it had lots of fast, hip-hop style rap numbers, and used hip-hop dance battle scene for the arguments. Although it was about political history of United States with main characters mostly consisted with White people, the show intentionally chosen to have actors and actresses in color. Using the unusal dance and number styles, it showed how energetic the historical figures were back in old days. It was interesting that the show made fun of Britain, using King George character as dumb, obsessive chararcter with the states. My friends explained to me that when it comes to historical stories, Americans like to make fun of Britain becasue of the colonization background.

How

Since I'm an exchange student only for the semester, I didn't have much supplies here. I didn't have TV or big screen, so I liked to borrow projectors, go to my friend's room, or just go to the theatre. I used streaming services like Amazon Video Prime, Disney Plus, Hulu, Netflix, etc. either with my own account or my friend's when I was watching movies with my friends. I don't have much knowledge about the streaming services in America, so let me introduce you to the streaming services in South Korea so maybe you can use it when you come to Korea.

  • 1: Netflix: It's actually the same as you use in the states, but the list of contents you can use might differ.
  • 2: Tving: It's good for live streaming shows or Korean TV series.
  • 3: Coupang Play: Coupang is like Amazon Shipping service in Korea, and it also has video streaming service like Amazon Video Prime. It's known for the live streaming service of sports games. If you have Coupang Wow Membership, you can use this for free.
  • 4: Disney Plus: Unlike American Disney Plus membership that usually comes with Hulu membership, we only have single Disney Plus membership. I could use my Disney Plus membership from Korea in US.
