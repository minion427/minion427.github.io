Website Evaluations

A List Apart

A List Apart focuses on web design and development, known for its clean, readable, and well-structured design.

  • Contrast: Excellent contrast between black/gray text and a white background. Links are clearly distinguishable.
  • Repetition: Consistent typography and color scheme across pages, with elements like the header and footer repeating throughout.
  • Alignment: All elements are well-aligned with clear, consistent spacing.
  • Proximity: Related elements such as navigation links and content are grouped logically, enhancing usability.

Zombo.com

Zombo.com is an intentionally chaotic website that lacks any basic design principles, but it provides a unique case study in "what not to do".

  • Contrast: Poor contrast with neon gradients and text that's hard to read, making the site visually overwhelming.
  • Repetition: Inconsistent typography and layout across pages. No repeated design elements or cohesive style.
  • Alignment: Misaligned text and elements make the page look disorganized. No structured design whatsoever.
  • Proximity: Information is scattered all over the page, with no logical grouping of related content. Navigation and content feel disconnected.
